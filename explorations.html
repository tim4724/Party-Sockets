design exploration · ascii · dos & htop

Six variants of the two surviving ASCII shapes.

Three takes on the DOS-windowed approach (palette and pane structure), three takes on the htop columnar density (single-pane, split-pane, compact). No load metric — there isn’t one to honestly back yet.
